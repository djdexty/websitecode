DEXTY

DJ & Producer

Dexty is a DJ & Music Producer from Košice, Slovakia. He currently works as a resident DJ in 4 venues.

THE STORY

Daniel Rolák (professionally known as Dexty) is a 17-year-old DJ from Košice, Slovakia. He began to devote himself to music at the age of 14 as a beatmaker, later he became interested in DJing. At the age of 15, he decided to become a DJ.

He played his first show at the Piano Club in Košice. After this show, he decided to play all over Slovakia. When he was 16, he played events in more than 20 cities. He also participated as a DJ at the Vibe Festival.

He currently works as a resident DJ in the following clubs: Boba Bar Košice, Jazz Caffé Košice, Zlatý Dvor Košice, Meduza Prešov. In a short time, Dexty managed to play more than 80 events and also acts as a DJ for a Slovak rapper named Deelay. Together they played concerts alongside names like Separ, Gleb, Ego, Pil C, etc.
